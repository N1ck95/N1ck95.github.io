About me

Hi, I am a Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) specialized in Emerging Digital Technologies, with a focus on Embedded Systems. In April 2024 I received my PhD in Emerging Digital Technologies at the School of Advanced Studies Sant'Anna (ReTiS Laboratory), under the supervision of Prof. Giorgio Buttazzo and Prof. Alessandro Biondi. My research focuses on improving the security and performance of I/O in Embedded Systems. From November 2022 to May 2023, I was a visiting PhD student at the PRECISE laboratory at the University of Pennsylvania, where I worked under the supervision of Prof. Linh Thi Xuan Phan. During this time, I contributed to developing a robust Intrusion Detection System for Cyber-Physical Systems, using machine learning techniques to counter zero-day vulnerabilities. In 2020, I graduated cum laude with a Master of Science in Embedded Computing Systems, a joint program offered by the School of Advanced Studies Sant'Anna (SSSA) and the University of Pisa. My master's thesis focused on predicting the spread of COVID-19. During my master’s program, I also participated in a cyber defense exercise alongside the cyber team of the Italian Army, where I was a member of the blue team. My primary responsibility was defending the IoT infrastructure. My main interests include Cybersecurity and Artificial Intelligence, with a particular passion for the Automotive sector.

Drone Follower

Real time simulator of a drone that have to follow a ball. The ball movement is generated randomly by a dedicated task. The simulator has to recognize and follow the ball using a virtual camera mounted on the drone. The visualization of the environment should be done in Unreal Engine, while all of the computation regarding image recognition, trajectory planning, drone control and system simulation have to be done by a separated program running on Linux using Allegro 3 Library.
